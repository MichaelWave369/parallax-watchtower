# Guardrails

Parallax Watchtower is a public-signal awareness tool. It must not become a stalking, doxxing, or private-surveillance system.

## Allowed

- Public and official source feeds
- Public news and market records
- Consent-based browser location
- User-created watch zones
- Confidence labels and receipt links

## Not allowed

- Hidden device tracking
- Private camera ingestion
- Face identification
- Person-targeting workflows
- Claims of causation from mere correlation

## Product rule

The Watchtower does not control the sea. It helps people see the conditions clearly.
